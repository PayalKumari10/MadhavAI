• Get personalized crop recommendations based on your soil, weather, and market conditions

**🌱 Fertilizer & Seed Guidance**
• Soil-based fertilizer recommendations
• Dosage and timing for optimal results

• Trusted seed sources and suppliers

**🏛️ Government Schemes Navigator**
• Discover schemes you're eligible for
• Step-by-step application guidance
• Required documents checklist
• Deadline reminders

**📚 Farming Education**
• Short, practical lessons (3-5 minutes)
• Voice narration in your language
• Topics: pest management, irrigation, organic farming, and more

**🔔 Smart Alerts & Reminders**
• Timely reminders for sowing, fertilizing, irrigation, and harvest

• Market price alerts

• All core features work without internet

**🗣️ Voice Interface**
